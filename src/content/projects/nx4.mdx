NX4 is a web-based visualization tool for the exploration of aligned viral sequences. The tool was born as an alternative to matrix-based MSA visualizations.
This is an open- source project maintained by a small group of web-developers, visualization researchers and computational scientists

### Role

Assume the role of web developer, including Dev-Ops, helping with the visualization tool and creating a development and deployed process and environments

### Achievements

Published an [article in Oxford Bioinformatics](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btz457) about the application and its features
