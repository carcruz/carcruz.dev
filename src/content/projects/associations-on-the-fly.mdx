Associations on the Fly is a breakthrough interactive tool that enables pharmaceutical researchers to dynamically adjust evidence weights when prioritizing drug targets. Published as lead author in Oxford Bioinformatics (2024), this tool processes 7.8 million target-disease associations from 20+ data sources, allowing real-time recalculation and visualization of association scores.

The tool addresses critical limitations in existing target prioritization workflows by providing researchers with the flexibility to weight evidence according to their specific research contexts and therapeutic areas.

### Role

**Lead Developer & Research Lead** - Responsible for conceptualization, methodology design, software architecture, full-stack development, and validation of the entire system.

### Technical Architecture

- **Frontend**: React/TypeScript with real-time scoring framework
- **Backend**: Python API for association recalculation and data processing  
- **Data Processing**: 7.8M target-disease associations from 20+ evidence sources
- **Performance**: Instant recalculation and visualization updates
- **Integration**: Seamlessly integrated into Open Targets Platform serving 50k+ monthly users

### Key Technical Challenges Solved

**Real-time Scoring at Scale** - Built framework allowing researchers to adjust evidence weights and see association scores recalculate instantly across millions of data points without performance degradation.

**Interactive Evidence Exploration** - Created intuitive interface for complex evidence weighting that doesn't require bioinformatics expertise, making advanced target prioritization accessible to broader research teams.

**Production Integration** - Successfully deployed as core feature of Open Targets Platform, maintaining 99.9% uptime while processing complex queries from pharmaceutical companies worldwide.

### Research Impact & Validation

- **Lead Author** publication in Oxford Bioinformatics (Impact Factor: 5.8)
- **Methodology Innovation** - Established new standard for interactive evidence exploration in drug discovery
- **Global Deployment** - Integrated into workflows at major pharmaceutical companies for target selection processes
- **Community Adoption** - Featured in Open Targets Platform serving drug discovery researchers globally

### Achievements
